Football

It is a team sport played between two
teams of 11 players each, who primarily
use their feet to propel a ball around a
rectangular field called a pitch.

Basket Ball

a game played between two teams
of five players in which goals are
scored by throwing a ball through a
netted hoop fixed at each end of the
court.

Gym & Fitness

A gym is a building or room that's meant
for playing indoor sports or exercising. You
might go to the gym to pump iron, or you
might go to the gym to see who else is
pumping iron.

Tennis

a game in which two or four players
strike a ball with rackets over a net
stretched across a court.The usual
form (originally called lawn tennis ) is
played with a felt-covered hollow rubber
ball on a grass, clay, or artificial surface.

Golf

a game played on a large open-air course, in
which a small hard ball is struck with a club
into a series of small holes in the ground,
the object being to use the fewest possible
strokes to complete the course.

Volley Ball

a game for two teams, usually of six
players, in which a large ball is hit by
hand over a high net, the aim being
to score points by making the ball
reach the ground on the opponent's
side of the court.
